[ PLATFORM OVERVIEW ]

Strike at Range.
Without Dependence.

The Tuve is a vertically-launched, electrically propelled one-way attack loitering munition. Powered by a patented CR Systems dual-axis contra-rotating motor and guided by a dual EO/IR seeker with an onboard AI autonomy stack, it finds, fixes, and finishes targets at 5 to 20 km with minimal operator input in fully GNSS and RF-denied conditions.

5–20 km Range
